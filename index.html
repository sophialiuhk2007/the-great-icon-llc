About The Great Icon LLC

The Great Icon LLC is a technology company specializing in verifiable credential solutions that prioritize user privacy and security. We develop innovative mobile applications that enable secure credential management while integrating seamlessly with Apple's ecosystem.

To provide users with secure, privacy-preserving digital credential solutions that put control back in their hands while ensuring seamless integration with trusted platforms like Apple Wallet.
